About me:

My name is Nolan J. Pilon. I was born July 3rd 2003 in Cornwall Ontario. I have recently graduated from La Citadelle highschool with a bilingual diploma. I am also an Ontario Scholar. I am studying Game Programming at St-Lawence College with hopes of using the skills I learn to become a better indie developer. I have worked on many prototypes and various small games that i have not published in the Unity3D engine. I have participated in three game jams so far and will continue doing so in the future. The three games I published are Apart, a puzzle platformer set in an abandoned lab, Skal, a set of micro games with a viking theme, and most recently, Into The Pit, a precision platformer with very little room for error. I have experience with many coding languages such as: HTML, CSS, C#, C++, and GML. I am most firmiliar with Unity3D however I have used Game Maker Studio and Unreal Engine in the past.


My hobies include making games, skateboarding, snowboarding, cooking, and playing games.
